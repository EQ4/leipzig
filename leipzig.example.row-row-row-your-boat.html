ektara
(ektara note gate dur decay coef noise-amp pre-amp amp distort rvb-mix rvb-room rvb-damp lp-freq lp-rq pan out-bus)
a stringed instrument synth with 1 strings mixed and sent thru
distortion and reverb effects followed by a low-pass filter.  Use
the pluck-strings and strum-strings helper functions to play the
instrument. Note: the strings need to be silenced with a gate -> 0
transition before a gate -> 1 transition activates it. This instrument
is transient.  When a string becomes silent, it will be freed.
